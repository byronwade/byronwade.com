[Rebuzzle](https://rebuzzle.byronwade.com) is a daily puzzle platform that generates hard puzzles using AI. Each day brings a new challenge designed to test your problem-solving skills.

## The Concept

Rebuzzle delivers:
- **Daily Challenges**: A new hard puzzle every day
- **AI-Generated**: Puzzles created by artificial intelligence for unique and varied challenges
- **Progressive Difficulty**: Puzzles designed to challenge and engage
- **Clean Interface**: Focus on the puzzle, nothing else

## Technology

Built with modern web technologies to deliver a fast, responsive puzzle experience:
- **Next.js** for optimal performance
- **AI Integration** for puzzle generation
- **Mobile-First** design for solving puzzles anywhere

## What's Next

Continuously improving the puzzle generation algorithms and adding new puzzle types to keep the challenges fresh and engaging.
