A client project for Impact Marine Group, a professional marine services company. This project focused on creating a modern, responsive website that effectively communicates their services and expertise.

## Project Overview

Impact Marine Group needed a website that:
- Showcases their marine services and capabilities
- Provides an easy way for customers to contact them
- Works seamlessly across all devices
- Reflects their professional brand

## Solution

Built a clean, modern website using:
- Responsive design that works on all devices
- Fast loading times and optimized performance
- Clear navigation and intuitive user experience
- Professional design that matches their brand

## Results

The website successfully represents Impact Marine Group's services and provides an excellent first impression for potential customers.
