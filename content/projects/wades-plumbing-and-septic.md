A client project for Wade's Plumbing and Septic, a professional plumbing and septic services company. This website was designed to help them reach customers and showcase their services.

## Project Overview

Wade's Plumbing and Septic needed a website that:
- Highlights their plumbing and septic services
- Makes it easy for customers to contact them
- Works well on mobile devices (where many customers search)
- Builds trust and credibility

## Solution

Created a professional website with:
- Mobile-first responsive design
- Clear service descriptions and contact information
- Fast loading times
- Professional appearance that builds trust

## Results

The website provides Wade's Plumbing and Septic with a strong online presence and an effective way to connect with customers.
